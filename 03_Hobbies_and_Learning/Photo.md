## Beyond the Basics: Amazing Things Your Digital Assistant Can See

Most of us are used to simple photo tools that can make a picture brighter or crop out the edges. But your Digital Assistant can do something much more incredible: **it can actually "look" at your photos and understand what is in them.**

This is called "Visual Intelligence," and it can be a huge help in your daily life, acting like a pair of expert eyes that can identify things for you in an instant.

## Identifying the World Around You

Have you ever seen a beautiful flower in the park or a strange bug in the garden and wondered, "What on earth is that?" Instead of guessing, you can simply show the photo to your Digital Assistant.

*   **Plant and Animal Identification:** Take a photo of a plant or a bird and ask: "What kind of flower is this, and how often should I water it?" or "What breed of dog is in this picture?"
*   **Identifying Landmarks:** If you have an old photo of a building from a trip years ago but can't remember the name of the city, ask: "Do you know where this building is located and what it is called?"
*   **Understanding Objects:** If you find a tool in the garage or a gadget in a kitchen drawer and aren't sure what it does, ask: "I've found this object. Can you tell me what it is and how I am supposed to use it?"

## Getting Help with Reading and Translation

For those of us who find small print frustrating or who travel to places with different languages, these "seeing" tools are a lifesaver.

*   **Reading Small Print:** If a medicine bottle or a restaurant menu has text that is too small to read, take a photo and ask: "Can you read the instructions on this bottle out loud for me?"
*   **Instant Translation:** If you are looking at a sign in a foreign language, simply take a photo and ask: "What does this sign say in English?"
*   **Summarizing Documents:** Take a photo of a long letter or a complicated contract and ask: "Can you look at this page and tell me the most important points in simple bullet points?"

## Creative Help with Your Photos

Your Digital Assistant can also give you ideas and advice based on what it sees in your pictures.

*   **Cooking from a Photo:** Take a picture of the ingredients in your refrigerator and ask: "I have these items. Based on the photo, what is a simple and healthy meal I can make for dinner tonight?"
*   **Fashion and Style Advice:** Take a photo of a piece of clothing you love and ask: "I really like this shirt. What colors of pants or shoes would go well with it?"

## Try this now!

Let's put these "expert eyes" to the test. Find something in your house that you are curious about—perhaps a houseplant, a piece of art, or a strange kitchen tool. Take a photo of it and ask your Digital Assistant:

**"I've attached a photo. Can you tell me what this is and give me two interesting facts about it?"**
